On this page

表现名词的特征与状态。一般在名词短语中以及补语位置。

组成 ​

一般由形容词或形容词短语组成。

所谓短语形式，是指包含了非形容词词性的单词构成的短句。

复合名词前面的名词也可当成形容词成分，此时该名词不表现其他名词性质，如不是复数。

a government store.
my pencil sharpener.

此时的名词若是短语形式， 则必须先加上-制造成复合形容词。

a turn-of-the-century publication.
a five-year-old child.

复合形容词通常由数词，形容词（分词），副词，名词通过-连接构成，如full-time，world-famous，hard-working，newly-made(新建的)，ten-year，three-legged，second-hand，three-year-old。

功能 ​

作定语前置修饰名词时，其语气是比较永久性的，直接表现名词的特征。

John is a sick man. // 强调其是久病的人

做表语，或后置形容词时，距离名词较远，一般表达名词的某些临时性、补充性的状态。

John is sick today and couldn't come to work. // 表述今天生病

可与介词构成介词短语，作副词，如for good(永远地)，for certain/sure，in short，in full(全部地)，on the whole。

位置 ​

形容词在名词短语中可放在名词的前面或后面。

前置修饰名词 ​

通常的，形容词在名词前面，限定词之后，即【限定词+形容词+名词】结构。

I am reading an interesting book.

多个形容词时，需要遵循一定的顺序规则：观点+特征+用途。

特征即描述事物本身属性的形容词，越接近事物本质的越靠近名词。一般地，有：

This beautiful, big, old, red, Chinese wooden table was my grandmother's.
a crystal, drinking glass.

当使用两个及以上颜色形容时，使用and连接。

a black and white dress
a red, white and green flag

后置修饰名词 ​

  • 修饰不定代词时，形容词要后置。

I have something important to say.
Someone else will have to do it.

  • a-开头的古英语的形容词，其代表一种暂时性的状态，一般也放在后面。

John and his brother alike are unreliable.
Money alone cannot solve our problem.

  • 表度量的形容词，如six meters long，five meters deep/high。

  • 句子构成的形容词短语，可以定语的成分位于名词之后，这可看作关系从句的简写。

the songs (which is) popular in the 1970s.

一般有以下几种形式：

a nation as(so) large as California.
parents eager to support their children's efforts.
a waiter busy serving the guests.
He bought a set of furniture, simple and beautiful.

有时，可以把定语成分的形容词提前。

a taller man than John. // a man taller than John

某些形容词前置时与后置时表达的含义不一样。

名词前置意义后置意义
adopted被收养的被采纳的
known知名的已知的
involved复杂的有关的

表语位置 ​

这个位置的形容词比较自由， 单词、短语皆可使用。

Chinese culture is 5000 years old.

a- 开头的古英语形容词，如alive、afloat、awake，一般也放在补语位置。

The fish is still alive.
The balloon stays afloat.

比较级 ​

有三种逻辑关系：

A is shorter than B.
A is less difficult than B.
A is as boring as B.

构成形式 ​

单音节的形容词，在词尾变化，即-er，-est，如taller，tallest。

三个音节的，采用前缀处理，即more，most，如more expensive, most expensive。

两个音节的形容词，如果词尾是典型的形容词词尾，如-ed、-ing、-ous、-ful、-ive，应采用前缀处理，如 more helpful、most helpful。其余则随意。

以-ly结尾的副词的比较级形式也采用前缀处理。

对于采取词尾变化的。

  • 以-y结尾时，应将其变为-ier/est。
  • 以-e结尾的，直接加-r/st。
  • 以【一个元音+辅音】结尾的，要先双写辅音字母，再加尾缀。

一些不规则形式的词：

有些比较级或最高级有另外的含义，可独立使用。

further/furthest：进一步的，如further discussion(继续讨论)，further information(更多的信息)。

elder：表示家庭成员之间的长幼关系，如elder brother(哥哥)，eldest son(长子)。比较级句子中一般用older。

later：较晚的/地。

Could you call me later?

latter：后者，与former对应。

Should we take a bus or a taxi? Kim wants to take the bus,but I prefer the latter.

latest：最近的，最新的，如the latest news(最新消息)。

last：在目前之前的一个，或一系列事物中的最后一个。

I like it much better than his last one.
He was the last to arrive.

一部分形容词没有比较级或最高级形式，通常表达【绝对的】含义，如absolute，final，alone，equal，perfect，single，empty。

可以通过一些副词弱化其【绝对的】含义，如nearly perfect(接近完美的)，almost fatal(几乎致命的)。

基础用法 ​

than ​

比较句中，than作为副词，根据比较的对象，后面可接任意的充分，如名词，形容词，副词，非谓语，句子等。

Tom is taller than Lucy.
He is more brave than wise.
We have had much more rain this year than last year.
Better to do well than to say well.
I admire and respect you more than I can say.

than后面接句子时经常省略掉主语或谓语，为了保持句子简单同时不改变原意。

Two eyes can see more than one (can see).
She is kinder than he was. // was 表达过去，不能去掉

对于than/as省略后仅剩人称代词的情况，一般用其宾格形式，或保留主谓形式。

You’re taller than me(I am).
I can’t run as fast as him(he can).

than常用词组 ​

None than：表达【仅仅，只是】，【完全是】，有，no/none/nothing other than，nothing more than，nothing else than(完全是)。

I can hardly be other than grateful to you.
What he told me was nothing else than nonsense.

no [-er] than：强调与比较级相反的含义，有no further than(只到)，no/litter less than(不少于，与...一样多)，no/litter better than(简直是)，no more than(至多，仅仅)。

Sally is no less charming than you.
He is no more than a puppet.

对于not [-er] than则强调较弱的比较级的性质。

其他的有：less/more than(少于，多于)，more A than B(与其说是B，不如说是A)，would rather/sooner... than...(宁愿...，也不...)，know better than to do sth(应懂得不做某事)。

Your son is more shy than unfriendly.
Your son is shy rather than unfriendly.
I would rather give up sleep than miss my evening class.

as...as ​

表达【与...一样】，第一个as为程度副词，通常可用so代替，第二个为副词，与than类似。否定形式为(not) so/as … as。

常用的结构是as adj/adv as，as adj a/an n as，as much/many n as。

He is as diligent a student as you.
I have as many books as you have.

as常用词组 ​

nothing so much as(极其)，not/without so much as(甚至...都没有)，as adj/adv as, if not better than,(即便不比...好，也至少和其差不多)。

I feared nothing so much as the sight of her.
His health is as good as, if not better than, his sister's.

adj to ​

某些形容词后接to也常于比较，如superior to(优于)，inferior to(低于)，prior to(先于)，senior to(年长于)。

His knowlegde of English literature is superior to mine.

by ​

by用于比较时可表达相差数量

He is taller than I by a head.
He is my senior by two years.

倍数 ​

表达倍数可通过n times，其中n>=3，对于2倍则一般用twice或double。

结合than或as...as的表达形式通常为n times adj than，n times as adj as，n times as a/an n as。

The box is twice bigger than that one.
He earns five times as much money as he did ten years ago.

可以认为此种方式是by n times的简写。

另一种形式为n times the n，【几倍于】，n后面可以有后置定语，或者what引导的定语从句。

He did it one-third the time it took me. The price is ten times what it normally is.

这种形式可认为是n times of the n的简写。

对象一致性 ​

比较的对象双方应保持一致。

My car is bigger than yours.

为了保持句子在修辞上的整洁与美观，一般会采取代指、倒装方式组织句子。

代指：利用that/those代替前面的名词。

Cars made in Taiwan are better than those made in Korea.

倒装：将从句中用于比较的助动词提到从句前面。

A chimp has as much I.Q. as does a child of five or six. // 这里的does用来指代has I.Q.。

其他用法 ​

  • 用一些词进行程度上的修饰：much，a lot，a little，any。

Don’t go by train. It’s a lot more expensive.
I’m not waiting any longer. (= not even a little longer)

像very, many, quite，fairly这些词一般不用来修饰比较级。

  • 表达动态改变的过程：【越来越...的】，如better and better, more and more。

It’s getting better and better.
More and more tourists are visiting this part of the country.

  • 表达意愿，递进，【越...，就越...】，用 the … the …。the...需在句中充当成分。

The sooner the better. (= as soon as possible)
The younger you are, the easier it is to learn.

  • 进行与类别或范围的比较时，注意将自身排除在外。借助any other n或something else。

最高级 ​

通常最高级通过介系词来表示的限定的范围内。用in接地方或组织，of接时间或其他。

The Huanghe River is the second longest river in China.
Yesterday was the hottest day of the year.

以of形式时，通常会省略掉one。还可以将of后的结构置于句首。

The youngest (one) of the family is most successful.
Of all metals, gold is the least useful.

not+比较级的形式也可以表达最高级的含义。

It could not be worse.
I have never heard a better song.

一些短语结构：at one's [-est](处于最...)，be of the [-est](十分的)，not in the least(一点也不)，the [-est] by far(最最...)。

The hat is of the most modern style.
It is the best film by far that I have even seen.

主动与被动 ​

-ing：令人...的，表达事物引起了他人某种感觉。通常用于物。

-ed：对某事...的，表达主体对某事的感觉。通常用于人。

the exciting news // 激动人心的消息
the excited man // 显得很激动的人

My job is boring.
I'm bored with my job.

-ing用于人，表达他人对此人的感觉。

He is interesting to listen to. // 听他讲话很有意思

-ed用于物，以主语的感觉形容此物。

Tom had a frightened look on his face. // 被吓坏了的表情
His tired voice. // 他疲惫的声音
