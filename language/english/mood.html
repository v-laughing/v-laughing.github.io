语气，用来表示说话者的意图和态度。英文中的语气可分为三种：陈述语气、祈使语气和虚拟语气。

虚拟语气 ​

一般来讲，英语中的虚拟语气可以分为两大体系。一是虚拟条件句，用来表示与事实相反或假想的情形，通常由 if 引导。另一个体系是在名词从句中使用虚拟语气，表示建议、命令或要求等语气。

虚拟条件句 ​

虚拟条件句有两种类型：假设条件句和事实相反句。前者表达的是一种在将来或现在主观认为可能性不大的情形，后者表达的是一种与现在或过去的事实相违背的情形。

显然，根据含义，对于不同的时间，可以表达的虚拟句类型有所不同。

  • 将来时间：只能表达假设条件句。
  • 现在时间：两者皆可。
  • 过去时间：只能表达事实相反句。

通常，虚拟条件句以主从结构表现出来。对于不同的时态，主从句的谓语动词变化如下：

将来虚拟

If I were to live my life over again, I would have you as my wife.
If I should win the lottery, I would buy a house.

对比：

If it were to rain, I would stay home. // 认为将来不太可能会下雨
If it rains, I will stay home. // 可能性大些

现在虚拟

If I were you, I would help him. // 事实相反
If I had the time now, I would help him. // 假设

过去虚拟

If I had had the time yesterday, I would have helped him.

混合虚拟 有时主句与从句的时态并不相同。

从过主现：

If you had married him, you would be the wife of a gas station.
If they had invested in that stock, they might be wealthy now.

从现主过：此时从句表达某事从过去到现在依然如此。

If I didn't love her, I wouldn't have married her. // 一直爱着
If I hadn't loved her, I wouldn't have married her. // 当时爱，现在不爱
If I didn't love her, I wouldn't marry her. // 打算娶她

倒转结构 ​

虚拟从句可以采用倒装结构。当 if 引导的条件句省去 if 时，可将 should, had 或 were 置于句首。

Were he to leave today, he would get there by Monday.
> Should you change your mind, no one would blame you.
> ** Had** he not been promoted, he would never have remained with thecompany.

其他结构 ​

上面介绍的主句与从句都是虚拟的。借助于一些连接词，如or、otherwise、but，可以以陈述句+虚拟句的结构表达虚拟语气，其中连接词从当了虚拟语句的从句。

We didn't know his telephone number, otherwise we would have telephoned him.
We would have telephoned him if we had known his telephone number.

He would put on weight, but he doesn't eat much.
He would put on weight if he ate much.

名词从句虚拟 ​

一类通过某些标志词来表达愿望、建议、命令、请求或意志等语气的名词从句也可归类为虚拟语句。在这种结构中，从句的谓语形式均为should+动词原形，其中should可以省略。

I suggest that we should go tomorrow.
I suggested that we should go the next day.

宾语从句 由以下词等所引导的宾语从句：ask, advise, beg, command, demand, decide,request, suggest和urge。

The instructions ask that we not take more than three tablets once.
The board deemed it urgent that these files should be printed rightaway.

it 引导的主语从句

It is strange that he should not come here. // 表惊讶
> It is important that the hotel receptionist make sure that guests areregistered correctly.

It is requested that all members be present at the meeting.

It is my proposal that he be sent to study further abroad.

表语从句

For my own part, it seems that the main requirement of an international language is that it be easily learned.

同位语从句

The suggestion that the mayor present the prizes was accepted byeveryone.

其他虚拟句 ​

wish ​

表达说话者对难以实现的或已发生过的事情的期望，后悔或祝愿，通常具有较强的感情色彩。

现在

I wish I were a little younger.
I wish I knew his address. // 实际不知道

过去

I wish I could have gone with you to the concert last night.

将来

I wish he could explain what he means.
I wish they would keep their squabbles to themselves.

if only ​

表达【但愿】，【要是……就好了】的意思。用法与wish基本相同，但比wish具有更强烈的感情色彩。

If only I were taller.
If only he had followed your advice!

可以将only放在句中。

If he had only followed your advice!

as if/as though ​

表达【好像】的意思。当然，仅当描述的事情基本不可能发生时才用虚拟语气。

It seems as if he has been to America. // 并没有

It is becoming dark. It looks as if it is going to rain. // 真的要下雨了

it's time that ​

从句谓语用过去时，暗含【早就该做某事的了】的意思。

it is about time we cleared it off.
It seems to be high time that this argument were put to an end.

would rather ​

【宁愿】之意。

would rather do sth

He would rather stay at home than go to the cinema tonight.
He would rather not stay at home tonight. // 否定句

would rather have done sth：宁愿自己做了某事（但实际上没有做）

I took Sally to the cinema last night, but I would rather have been there alone.

would rather sb. did

Don't come tomorrow. I'd rather you came next weekend.

would rather sb. had done

I would rather my father had given me a set oftransformers.

此类表述还有would as soon, would sooner和would prefer。

Our holidays were ruined by the weather — we might just as well have stayed at home.
