基本句型 ​

描述行为 ​

表达主语发生的行为。

S+V

html
He died. I swim.

S+V+O

html
John Smith killed three enemy soldiers.

S+V+O+O 双宾语，前一个表示动作承受的对象，后一个表示动作采取的对象。

html
John's father gave him a dog.

描述特征 ​

表达某个对象的特征：补语，通常是形容词或名词。

S+V+C 主语补语。这里的 V 为连缀动词，表示主语的特征具有某种动作的具象化。如：

V释义V释义
Look看起来seem似乎是
turn转变为prove证实为
become成为make做为

对于 Be 动词，它没有表达出一个具体的动作，仅仅描述了主语具有什么特征。因此，上述表中的动词可以看作有具体动作的 Be 动词。

html
Taroko Gorge is beautiful. The dog seems friendly. He became a teacher. A nurse
makes a good wife. // 娶护士做太太真不错

S+V+(O+C) 宾语补语。主语通过动作表达某个对象具有怎样的特征。特征可能是该动作引起的或对象固有的。

html
The meat made the dog friendly. // 肉让狗变得很友善 I consider the story false.
// 我认为故事是捏造的 He found the trip exciting. // 他觉得这次旅行很刺激

名词 ​

一个名词短语可由三部分构成：限定词* 形容词* 名词。限定词决定了名词的范围，形容词表达了名词的特征。

html
many good students 许多好学生 his wife 他美丽的妻子 //省略形容词 the best answer
最好的答案

可数与不可数 ​

可数名词有单复数形式，单数形式需配合限定词使用，如a/the/my，复数形式与不可数名词都可单独使用。

a student，a bowl of rice，some music，much shopping

一个名词并非只能可数或不可数，不同的含义有不同的可数性质。

This is a nice room. （房间）
You can’t sit here. There isn’t room. （空间）

省略，常用的一些不可数名词会直接用限定词修饰：a (cup of) coffer/tea

单复数 ​

复数形式可来表达一个事物的两部分，如glasses，pants。

单数形式说明将该名词视为一个整体，像Fifty thousand pounds，three years。

单数形式的集体名词可表达复数的意思，如audience， committee ，company， family，即其谓语可用复数，也可用单数。

The police are investigating the crime. (not is)

一般地，单数用person，复数用people。

代词 ​

人称代词 ​

myself，yourself，ourselves

Steve introduced himself.

一般不用在wash/dress/shave/relax后面。

He got up, washed, shaved and dressed.

可以起到强调自身的意味。

I repaired it myself.
The film itself wasn’t very good, but I loved the music.

by myself：alone，on my own

Some people prefer to live by themselves(on their own).

somebody ​

somebody/nobody 等为单数形式，然而their/they表达他们。

Someone has forgotten their umbrella.

nobody ​

no noun == none == not a noun

No reason was given for the change of plan.
This money is all yours. None of it is mine.

none of + 复数名词，其谓语可用复数或单数。

nobody == not anybody

I said nothing. (I didn’t say anything.)

限定词 ​

当一个名词可指定范围并且想指定范围之时，便用限定词。没有限定词的名词短语表达该名词宽泛的含义。

Unmarried men are a rare species these days.

a(n) 表达在多个对象中的某几个。

Mr. Elson is also a member of the Council of the city.

the 表达在多个对象中特指的一个。

The history of recent China is a sorry record.
I'm going to the office now.

显然，对于专有名词，抽象名词等都不应该加 a(n)或 the。

值得注意的，对于像the Pacific(太平洋)等“专有名词”，可以解释为the Pacific Ocean——那个叫做 Pacific 的洋。

表达** “某种类型的” **

The giraffe is the tallest of all animals.
> The piano is my favourite instrument.

the + adj：可做名词，表达一组/类事物，谓语用复数。

We need to do more to help the homeless.
The French are famous for their food.

所有格 ​

一般的，生物用S's表示，非生物用of S介系词短语形式来表示。

‘s 以 s 结尾的复数名词，用s'，如sisters’，the Carters’。

多个名词时，仅对最后一个添加所有格，如Jack and Karen’s。

地点，时间的所有格：the city’s streets， the world’s population，today’s newspaper，a week’s holiday

复合名词 ​

nonu + nonu：前一个名词充当形容词的作用，只能是单数形式。

时态 ​

描述动作发生的时间。分为简单式与完成式。前者强调动作发生的时间段(t1,t2)，后者强调动作的截至时间(, t)。

简单式 ​

  • 过去：表达动作发生在过去的某段时间。动作变为过去式，即-ed 形式。
  • 现在：动词不变。

    真理与事实表达了现在这段时间的事物，所以用现在时态。

  • 未来：动词变为 will be 形式。
html
//过去 The movable print was introduced to England in 1485. I was watching TV
when I heard the doorbell. //现在 All mothers love their children. 7-ELEVEN is
selling big cokes at a discount this mouth. //未来 The building will be razed
next month. Don't call me at six tomorrow.I'll still be sleeping then.

完成式 ​

表达动作持续了一段时间，并且在某个时刻截至。“已经”、“有过”之意。

  • 过去：had Ven 形式。
  • 现在：have Ven 形式。

    常见的一些时间特征有：for a week（到现在有一星期之久）、since（自从那时，到现在）、so far (到目前为止）

  • 未来：will have Ven 形式。
html
//过去 Many soldiers had died from pneumonia before the discovecry of penicilin.
// 现在 I'm sure I have seen this face somewhere. We have been working overtime
for a week to fill your order. //未来 Next April, I will have worked here for 20
years. Come back at 5:00.Your car will have been fixed by then.

不定式短语 ​

to v：可充当多种词类，如名词、形容词、副词。 可以认为，不定词短语由带有助动词的从句省略而来。

例如： I am glad because I can know you. —> I am glad to know you.

助动词通常由以下几点特征。

  1. 后面接原形动词。
  2. 带有不确定的语气。
  3. 用完成式来表达相对的过去时间。

    助动词本身都无法完整表达过去时间。因为 may 与 might 的区别仅仅是语气的强弱，而其后必须接动词原形。因此，当表示过去时，采用完成时。如，It must have rained last night。

于是，不定时短语使用的场景也应该具有以上特征之一。助动词与不定式短语通常有以下对照：

助动词不定式
musthave to
shouldought to
will/wouldbe going to
can/couldbe able to
may/mightbe likely to

因此，不定式短语通常跟在具有主观意义的动词后面，充当宾语。如，plan, want, try, hate, like 等等。

使役动词？ ​

动名词 ​

ving：充当名词类，以名词的形式来介绍某种带有持续性的动作。 可以认为，动名词由名词从句省略而来。

如：

html
That anybody picks strawberries can be fun. —> Picking strawberries can be fun.
<br />

与不定式短语充当名词时的区别主要有两点：

  • 强调动作的持续性，而非不确定性。
  • 可以有限定词或复数。

复合字 ​

将动名词改为复合字，此时宾语提到形容词的位置，没有单复数。

Picking strawberries -> Strawberry-picking

所有格 ​

从句的主语不能省略时，保留动名词的所有格。

html
I don't like that John calls my girlfriend day after day. -> I don't like John's
calling my girlfriend day after day. <br />

被动式 ​

Being Ven。

html
Being invited here is a great honor.

分词 ​

Ven、Ving：形容词类，描述对象特征。 Ven一般具有“被”，“已经”之意，而Ving有“正在”，“一直”之意。

html
The dog is barking. // 正在叫的 Boiled water is safe to drink. // 被煮开的 It is
gone. // 已经不见了的

辨析 ​

表达感觉 ​

html
He is disappointed at his scores. His scores are disappointing.

上述意思还原为：

html
His scores disappoint him.

因此，如果用 He 做主语，应该是被动态；用 His scores 做主语，应该用主动态。

副词 ​

主要用于表现动词的状态。可修饰名词、动词、形容词与副词。与形容词一样，有比较级。

语气 ​

利用动词变化来表达真、假口吻的方式。

叙述事实 ​

表示所说的是真的。

条件语气 ​

表示真假还不能确定。以语气助动词（如： must、 should、 will/would、 can/could、
may/might 等)来表达不确定的语气。

would等并不表示过去，而是表达更不确定的语气。另外，助动词后面需接动词原型。基于此，但表示过去的不确定语气时，用完成时表达。

It may have rained a little last night.

假设语气 ​

说反话，表示所说的与事实相反。以动词的过去形态表达假设的语气。 假设语句中，常包含不确定的主句成分，以助动词表示。

If only I had more time!
It's time you kids were in bed

假设的时间若是现在则退为过去，若是过去则退为过去完成式形态。

If I were you, I wouldn't do it.
If I had known earlier, I might have done something.

对未来事件的假设，由于其本质是不确定的，应该用助动词修饰。

If an asteroid should hit the earth, man could die out.

当强调未来事件绝不可能发生时，可用were to.

If I were to take the bribe, I could never look at other people in the
eye again.

祈使语气 ​

命令句，表示希望能成真，但尚未实现。以动词原型来表达祈使语气。

Leave the courtroom !

间接命令句：如It is necessary that... ，I insist that...。

主语动词一致性 ​

讨论动词的单复数问题。若主语是第三人称单数， 动词在现在时态中要加-s。

单复数问题 ​

限定词辨别：独立的名词一般有限定词修饰。

The senator and delegate (单)
The senator and the delegate (双)

整体描述：其对应的名词采用单数。

Every man and every woman is
All work and no play is

对于every、 each、 either、 neither等表示“一”的字眼，其后接的名词用单数，作主语时动词也如此。

Each of you is responsible for half of the job.

对于集体名词，如 family、 police、 committee、 crew，当表示“一个单位、集团”时用单数。若表示单位内的“成员”时，要用复数动词。

The committee are mostly Republican politicians.
The committee is five years old.

主语辨别 ​

当主语中含有连接词，如 and，or 等时，辨别动词应该对应的主语。

主句还原：一些连接词充当状语的成分。

But I, as well as you, am broke.
Mrs.Lindsey, together with her sons, is on a European tour

主语侧重：一些连接词充当与主语对比成分。

Not only you but also I was at fault.
The eggs, not the hen, were stolen.

二选一： 选靠近动词的部分做主语。

Either my father alone or both my parents are coming.

关系代名词 ​

作主语时取决于其代表的先行词。

It was the Johnson boys who were here last night.

空泛化主语 ​

其只表达“全部/部分”的概念，单复数根据介系词短语来判断。

All of these are Lishan pears.
All of the money has been spent.
None of the pears is/are really good to eat.

介系词辨析

a number/the number：前者表“某个数量的”，用复数。后者表“...的一个数目”，用单数。

a pair of：表示一对，用单数。

复合句 ​

合句：两个各自独立的简单句，以 and、 but、 or 等连接词连起来，没有主、从之分。

Girls like dolls, but boys like robots

复句：将一个句子改造成名词、形容词或副词类， 放到另一句中使用。

名词从句 ​

从句本身是完整的句子，在主句中充当名词的成分，可以放在主语、宾语、补语等位置。用连接词that将从句连接起来。

That he didn’ t show up on time is strange.

that 省略：在不影响意思的情况下，可将其省略。

The defendant said (that) he didn't do it.

it 代替：若从句较长，造成句子结构不清楚，可用 it 代替从句。

I find it strange that he didn't show up on time.
It is that ...

介系词省略：一些情况下，介词后跟从句时，介词可省略。

I am afraid (of) that I can't help you.

从句放大：句子侧重表达从句内容，可将主句放置后面，用逗号隔开。

This is your last offer, I suppose?

疑问句从句：疑问句为从句时，不需要that连接词。

When the bomb will go off is anybody's guess.

whether/if：疑问词，表“是否”。在句首位置以及介系词后面时只能用 whether。

  • 句首用if会使得句子看上去像疑问句。
  • 介系词后面接名词短语，而whether是which和either的合成词。

I can't tell whether/if he’ s telling the truth or not.

副词从句 ​

从句充当状语，用来修饰主句的动词，是一个完整的句子。使用连接词来表达主从之间的逻辑关系。

时间、地方

It was all over when I got there.
A small town grew where three roads met.

条件

He won't have it his way, as long as I'm here. (只要)

原因，结果

There's nothing to worry about, now that Father is back. (既然)
He looked so sincere that no one doubted his story. (所以)

目的

为了：in order that、so thatlest（以免）、in case(万一)

让步

While the disease is not fatal, it can be very dangerous. (虽然)
Whoever (=No matter who) calls, I won't answer.

限制

Picasso was a revolutionary in that he broke all traditions. （在于）

方法、状态

He played the piano as Horowitz would have. （如同）

关系从句 ​

在主句中充当形容词成分，对代名词或副词的修饰或补充说明。用关系词指代修饰的对象，同时连接主句。

I don't trust people who talk too much.

关系词省略：以不影响句意为准。一般情况下，关系词作宾语时可省略。

The man (whom) you saw just now is my uncle.

that 与 who/which：that 含有指示的功能。

Man is an animal ** that** is capable of reason.

对于起到补充功能的从句或介系词后面，不用that，也不能省略关系词，且需要用逗号隔开。that 前面也可以有逗号。

For boyfriend, I'm considering your brother John , who is tall.
Shakespeare was born in 1564，when Queen Elizabeth I was on the throne.

先行词省略：当先行词为空泛的概念时，可以将其省略，此时从句可看作名词从句。

I have what you need.
You can take whichever car you like.

关系副词：若先行词为副词类，一般可将其中之一（先行词或关系副词）省略；若其为名词，则不可省略；被省略时应当解释为名词从句。

The rain came (at a time) (when) the farmers needed it most.
The Johnsons have a place (where) they can get away from other people.
I know when he will arrive.

how/why

Can you show me the way (how) you pulled off that trick.
I've forgotten the reason (why) I called.

对等连接 ​

以对等连接词，如and、or、but将句子或短语连接起来，达到并列或对比的目的。要求连接的双方在结构与内容上对称。

not only... but also：两者所引导的部分应对称。

Not only is China the world's most populous state but it is also the
largest market in the 21st century.

分号：可以取代连接词来连接对等句子，通过副词来说明两句子的逻辑关系。

He's not an atheist ; rather, he believes in agnosticism.

从句简化 ​

删除从句间相对应位置中重复的部分，使句子更加精简。

基本通则 ​

通则：省略主语与 be 动词，只留补语。

省略主语：空洞的字眼(one、 everybody, people 等)，或者从句中主语重复出现，可省略从句中的主语。

It is common courtesy that one should wear black while one attends a funeral.
-> It is common courtesy to wear black while attending a funeral.

省略 be 动词：be 动词只扮演连接主语与补语的角色，没有实际意义。

其他情况：句子没有 be 动词时。

有助动词时，变成不定词。

You must go at once.
→You are to go at once.

替换后，表达的意思可能不是很精确，可以借助介系词短语来弥补。

He studied hard so that he could get a scholarship.
-> He studied hard in order to get a scholarship.

没有助动词时，变成 Ving。

John remembers that he saw the lady before.
John remembers seeing the lady before.

形容词从句 ​

若关系从句中简单的S+V+C结构，即 be 动词及补语的形式，可直接省略主语和 be 动词。

The change of style in these paintings should be obvious to anyone (who is) familiar with the artist's works.
Bill Clinton, (who is) President of the U.S.,is a Baby Boomer. (同位语)

可充当形容词的短语有三类：Ven、Ving、to V。对于一般动词，形容词从句可简化为这三类。

Ven：关系从句是被动态时。

Beer (which is) chilled to 6° C is most delicious.

Ving：关系从句是进行式，或没有 be 动词及助动词时。

My old car, (which breaks) breaking down every other week, won't last much longer.

to V： 关系从句有语气助动词时。

John is the one (who should go) to go this time.

若主从句主语不一致，可介系词来表现从句的主语。

I have a job for your brother to do.

名词从句 ​

一般会将无意义的 that 省略掉。

可以充当名词的短语有两类：Ving，to V。

Ving

He got used to working late into the night.
Many husbands enjoy doing the cooking.

主语不一致时：可用宾补、所有格、介系词的形式处理。

I imagined (that) a beautiful girl (was) singing to me.
His calling my girlfriend every day is too much for me.
I am worried about my son lying all the time.

处理被动态：借助 be 动词来表现名词的功能。

That anyone is called a liar is the greatest insult.
-> Being Called a liar is the greatest insult.

当从句是简单的S+V+C结构时，也借助 be 来表现名词。

That one is a teacher requires a lot of patience.
-> Being a teacher requires a lot of patience.

to V 与Ving比较，带有不确定的含义，用来表达某种意愿。

The children expect to get presents for Christmas.
I think it strange to fear ghosts.

主语不一致时

I want you to go. （宾补结构）
For the Clippers to beat the Lakers was quite a marvelous feat. (介系词结构)

疑问句 此时其连接词，如 when、how 是有意义的，故不能省略。

I dont know what I should do.
-> I don't know what to do.

副词从句 ​

对于简单的S+V+C结构，可以直接去掉主语和 be 动词，此时连接词应当保留。

While (it is) small in size, the company is very competitive.

Ving ​

副词从句是一般的进行式没有助动词的主动语态时，可改写为Ving形式。对于连接词的去留，看其省略是否会影响题意而定。一般地，对于 because、 since 等表原因的，可以将其省略。

当去掉连接词时，此时分词短语构成一个从句，此即为分词构句。

(while) Lying on the couch, the boy fell asleep.
Being a student, I can't afford to get married.

对于可作为介系词的连接词，如 before, after, since，当省略从句主语时，连接词会容易被理解为介系词，此时其后面应该接名词。

Before being in school, he used to be a naughty child.

主语不一致时，需要保留主语。此时有两点需要注意：连接词要省略；动词改写为分词形式。这样子从句构成了所谓的独立短语。

(When) The child already sleeping soundly in bed, her mother came to kiss her goodnight.

Ven ​

从句是被动态时。过去分词含有完成的含义，可省略掉同意的连接词，如After。

(After) Shot in the knee, he couldn't fight.

to V ​

从句中有语气助动词，带有不确定语气，简化之后就会成为不定词。

I am glad to help. （if i can help）
He studied hard in order to get a scholarship.

介系词短语 ​

可将从句改为介系词短语，连接词用意思相近的介系词代替，而从句部分有名词代替。例如，有：

when -> on、upon <br />
because -> because of <br />
although -> despite、in spite of <br />
if -> in case of <br />

从句部分的名词代替，一般有两种方式：动名词、动词的名词形式。

Upon completing the project, she was promoted.
Upon completion of the project,she was promoted.

对于主语不一致的情况。

With the exam (is) only a week away,I have no time to waste.

倒装句 ​

强调语气、增强清楚性与简洁性，以及更流畅地衔接前后的句子。主要通过助动词前置实现倒装。

比较级 ​

当从句中的助动词或 be 动词不宜省略，且主语后面有比较长的修饰语。

Girls like cats more than do boys, who as a rule are a cruel lot.

关系从句 ​

当指代先行词的关系词与其修饰的动词相距较远时。

The President is a man on whom falls a heavy responsibility. (responsibility falls on him)

假设语气 ​

在假设语气的副词从句中（往往是由 if 引导的)，如果有 be 动词或助动词，就可以考虑倒装。

Had I been there, I could have done something to help. （if i had been there...）

引用句 ​

突出强调内容的从句。

"None was killed in the accident. " said the police. (直接引用)
Cholera, warns the WHO, is coming back. (间接引用)

there is/are ​

地点副词向前移， 强调语气。

**Here** is your ticket for the opera! <br />
**To the west of Taiwan** lies Southern China. <br />

否定副词 ​

充当否定意味的副词开头且其修饰动词时，需要用倒装句。

Not until you are married will I stop waiting for you.
Hardly had I sat down to work when the phone rang.

修饰主语时，不需倒装。

Only I saw him yesterday.

not only , but also：需要保持结构的对称。

Not only did he pass the exam but he also scored at the top.

疑问句 ​

词语辨析 ​

副词或形容词 ​

well&good ​

  • 一般地，good 为形容词，well 为副词。well 作形容词时表健康良好的。
  • well 与过去分词搭配，组成形容词，如well-known、well-educated。

fast, hard & late ​

皆可充当形容词与副词，lately表最近的。

hard & hardly ​

  • 艰难地；几乎不。
  • hardly 一般在修饰词前面，如hardly know、hardly anyone、hardly a crisis。

so & such ​

  • 副词 & 形容词。表如此的、这样的。前者修饰形容词或副词，后者为名词。如，so tired、such a good time。
  • 一般两者可等价叙述：so far、such a long way。

enough & too ​

  • 足够的；过于。
  • adv: hard enough、long enough; adj: enough food;
  • 副词 too 在前面：too hard。too hot to eat：cannot eat。

quite, pretty, rather and fairly ​

相当地。程度低于very，高于a litter。

quite 较为通用； pretty 较口语化，其后一般接名词或形容词。

  • an 位置：quite a good job VS a pretty good job。
  • 用quite：quite a/an + noun、quite a lot of 、quite + verb。
  • quite 有完全地之意：quite true/abvious/agree.

rather 一般修饰消极的事物；用于积极的事物时，表惊讶的。

The weather isn’t so good. It’s rather cloudy.
These oranges are rather good.

fairly 语气程度低于前者。

older and elder ​

更老的。

  • elder 仅用于表示家庭中年长的一员，如My elder sister；不用于比较级。
  • older 均可表示。

still, any more, yet, already ​

still 指事物持续中，没有改变；而 not … any more 表事物已经改变。

When I went to bed, Chris was still working.
Lucy doesn’t work here any more. (Lucy no longer works here)

yet：还，已经。主要用于否定句和疑问句中，通常与现在完成时出现，表达说话者的期望。

It’s 10 o’clock and Joe isn’t here yet.
Mike lost his job six months ago and hasn’t found another job yet.

still也可用于否定句中，此时表达说话者的惊讶。

I sent him an invitation weeks ago and he still hasn’t replied.

already强调某些事早于预期发生。

I’ve just had lunch and I’m already hungry.

even ​

甚至。表达某事物是出乎意料的，惊讶的。

He always wears a coat, even in hot weather.
I can’t cook. I can’t even boil an egg.
I got up very early, but Jack got up even earlier.

即使：even though/even when/even if 状语从句连接词。

Even though Tina can’t drive, she has a car.

although ... ​

虽然，即使。

though，although，even though 有相近的意思，都充当状语从句的连接词。

Although it rained a lot, they had a good time.

though在口语中，通常将其放在句尾。

The house isn’t so nice. I like the garden though.

in spite of / despite 为介系词（短语），其后接名词。

In spite of the rain, we had a good time.

in case ​

万一。解释做某事的原因。连接词。

I’d better write down my password in case I forget it.

in case of = if there is …

In case of fire, please leave the building as quickly as possible.

unless & as long as ​

除非；只要。条件状语连接词。

unless：除非，表达另一种情况。意同except if.

You can’t go in unless you are a member.

as long as：只要。表达在某个条件下。同provided/providing。

You can borrow my car as long as you promise not to drive too fast.

as & when ​

as：修饰瞬时动作时，表当...时；修饰持续动作时，表随着。两个动作发生在同一时刻。

As I walked along the street, I looked in the shop windows.
As the day went on, the weather got worse.

when：修饰瞬时动作时，表在...之后，同after；修饰持续动作时，表在...期间，同during the time。

as 也有因为之意，同because、since。

like & as ​

like: 介词，像...的（东西）。副词结构，修饰名词，名词经常被省略。也作例如之意，同for example、such as。

What a beautiful house! It’s like a palace. （a place）
I enjoy water sports, like surfing, scuba diving and water-skiing.

as：连接词，如同。也有任何事之意，同what。

I didn’t move anything. I left everything as it was。
You’re late as usual/as always
Andy failed his driving test, as he expected.
You can do as(what) you like.

用作介词，作为之意。

As a taxi driver, I spend most of my working life in a car.

like & as if ​

像...的/地。引导形容词或副词从句。

作形容词时，通常跟在look, sound , feel后面。

You look like/as if you haven’t slept.
It sounds like/as if they’re having a party.

作副词时，突出动作的表现。as if引导的一般为虚拟语气，其中需要使用过去式表达现在式。like一般不使用为虚拟语气。

I don’t like him. He talks as if he knew everything.
He ran like he was running for his life.

范围 ​

both & neither & either ​

形容两个事物，同样有both与both of两种形式。

Both of these restaurants are good.
> Neither restaurant is expensive.

both及either对应的谓语用复数，而neither可用单数或复数。

写清楚两个事物时：

I was both tired and hungry when I arrived home.
> Neither Chris nor Paul came to the party.
> Either you apologise, or I’ll never speak to you again.

all & whole... ​

all其他用法

We were all happy. (强调)
He knows all about computers.
All I’ve eaten today is a banana. （the only thing）

whole：整个的，一般形容可数单数名词，放在限定词后面。

I read the whole book.

every：每个的，表达多久一次。

There’s a bus every ten minutes.

everyone & every one ​

前者只用来表示人，后者可表示人或事。

Sarah is invited to lots of parties and she goes to every one.

时间 ​

during & while ​

在...期间。

during 为介词，while 为时间状语连接词。

I fell asleep during the movie (while I was watching movie).

for：描述动作持续多长时间。

We watched TV for two hours last night.

by & until ​

在..之前；直到...

Joe will be back by Monday.
Joe will be away until Monday.

by the time...：在那时之前

By the time we get there, it will be closed.

at, on, in ​

一般地，表达在何时：

at for the time of day：at 11.45 ，at midnight。 on for days and dates：on Friday, on New Year’s Day。 in for longer periods：in the past, in June。

at：at the moment, at the same time, at night 。

morning/afternoon/evening: in the morning == on Friday morning。

no at/on/in before last/next：next week。

leave out on before days： on Friday == Friday。

in：在...时间内

The train will be leaving in a few minutes.
I learnt to drive in four weeks.

on time & in time ​

准时地；尽快地。

The 11.45 train left on time.
I sent Amy a birthday present. I hope it arrives in time.

just in time ：almost too late

We got to the station just in time for our train.

at/in the end ​

在...的结尾之时；最终（finally）。

At the end of the concert, everyone applauded.
We had a lot of problems with our car. We sold it in the end.

其他 ​

at, on, in ​

in

  • 处于...下：in the rain/sun，in a bad mood
  • other：in my opinion，in dollars，in language

on：on TV/phone，on holiday/vacation/trip

That car is on fire.
I didn’t do it on purpose.

at：at 100 degrees，at the age of

by ​

事情如何发生：by chance/accident/mistake

We hadn’t arranged to meet. We met by chance.

通过... ：by car/plane，pay by card 一般为非特指，不需要在名词前加定冠词。

被...：bitten by a dog

由...：a play by Shakespeare

紧接着的

The light switch is by the door.

表现差值

Clare’s salary has increased by ten per cent.

介词基本搭配 ​

noun + prep ​

此时介系词作形容词，意为 对于...的。

for：demand/reason for

of：advantage/picture/cause of

in：an invitation/key/solution to ， damage to

with：a relationship with

adj + prep ​

此时介系词作副词，意为 对...。

about / with：angry/excited/pleased about/with

about something == with somebody for doing something

at / by：surprised/shocked at/by

of：tired/afraid/proud/aware/full/capable of

for：sorry for/about，famous for

verb + prep ​

介系词作副词，意为 对...。

to(对...)：talk/speak/apologise/explain to

at：laugh/aim/look at

for(为...)：wait/apply/search for

about：talk/care/dream/complain/remind about

of：die of(死于)，consist of(由...组成)，accuse of(因...控诉)

on：depend on(依赖...)，spend (money) on

in：believe in(相信...存在)

about & of ​

I dreamt about you last night. (梦见...)
Do you dream of being rich and famous. (梦想着...)

I will think about what you said. （考虑...）
It was my idea. I thought of it first. (想到...)

We complained to the manager about the food. (抱怨，因不满意)
George was complaining of a pain in his stomach. (诉说)

It’s good you reminded me about the meeting. (提醒...)
This house reminds me of the one I lived in when I was a child. (使人想起...)

动词短语 ​

动词+介词（一般是有方向意义的）构成动词短语，介词来更具体地表现该动作。可以将其看成独立的一个动词，可以是及物或不及物。

We looked up at the plane. （不及物）
I turned on the light. (及物)

I turned on the light == I turned the light on. but only turned them on.

in：into something。plug in(插入)，drop in(随意访问)，move in(搬进)。

out ​

out of something：eat out(在外面吃)，drop out(退学)，get out(逃避，离开)

not burning, not shining：go out(熄灭)，put out(扑灭)

other：try out(实验)，carry out(执行)，run out(of...用光)

on&off ​

for lights, machines：turn/switch on/off(开关)

for events：call off(取消)，put off(延迟)

for clothes：put on(穿上)，take off(脱掉)，try on(试穿)

off(away from ...)：set off(出发)，take off(起飞)，see sb off(送人走)

on(continue doing sth)：go/keep on(继续)，play/walk on

other：get on(继续，with...与相处，) ，show off(炫耀)，rip off(欺骗)，tell sb off(责备某人)

up/down ​

direction：stand up(站着)，pick up(捡起来)，put down(放下)，put up/take down(挂起/拿下)

down(less, nagetive, stop)：let sb down(令人失望)，turn down(拒绝)，shut down(关闭)

up

approach：catch up(赶上)，keep up(跟上)

start：set up(建立)，take up(着手)，fix up(安排)

grow：bring up(抚养)，grow up(长大)

do/done：clean/wash/tidy up，use up(用完)

out：bring/come up(提出，提及)，make sth up(编造)，blew up(爆炸)，beat sb up(殴打)

other：end up(结束，with/in/doing/以...)，take up(占用)，put up(with...忍受)，mix up(混淆)
