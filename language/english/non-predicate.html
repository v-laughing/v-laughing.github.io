动词的一种变体，在句子中充当某种充分，不能充当完整的句子。

不定式 ​

to do sth可以当作名词、形容词、副词，在句中可充当除谓语之外的任何成分。

不定词通常可以认为由带有助动词的从句省略而来，表达【不确定】或【将来】的语气。

当名词 ​

作主语 ​

常见的是结构是，It + to do sth，可以与It + that sb can/want do sth对比。句子的逻辑主语可以通过for给出。

It is impossible for us to master English in a short time.
> It takes time and effort to master English.

不常见的一种是，不定式置于句首，通常是格言之类的句子。

To love for the sake of being loved is human, but to love for the sake of loving is angelic.

作表语 ​

可以表达建议、意愿或目的。

The important thing is to save lives.
The point is how to control yourself.

作宾语 ​

此时句子的谓语通常是描述态度的动词，如want，hope，refuse，promise。（因后面的宾语从句带有助动词）

由that引导的宾语从句带来的有：

V + to do

I hope to see you again.
He promised not to tell anyone about it.

V + 介词 + to do

I am accouting on all of you to help me through.

V + it + 宾补 + to do

He feels it challenging to be a marketing supervisor.

其他连接词引导的宾语从句带有的V + 连接词 + to do结构。

I wonder who to invite.　// who I should invite
Show us what to do.　// what we must do

作宾补 ​

即常见的V + sb + to do结构。

Allow me to drink to your success!
The chairman declared the meeting to be over.

另外，在let，make，have，see，hear，feel，watch，notice，listen to（使役动词与感官动词）等动词后面的不定式作宾语补足语的句子中，to要省略。

have/make sb do 区别

而hope，demand，suggest等词后面不可接不定式。

I hope/suggest that you can come on time.

当形容词 ​

即不定式充当定语，修饰名词，等同于定语从句的省略。这里需要注意的是不定式与被修饰名词在从句中的关系。

宾语关系 ​

I gave the kid a comic to read.
I had no place to live in. // 注意末尾介词
I have no language partner to practice speaking English with.

主语关系 ​

The man to help you is Mr.Johnson.
I don't think he is the best man to do the job.

状语关系 ​

Role playing is a safe and enjoyable way to learn English. // in which you can
The time to go is July. // at which you should go
A good place to eat is the Sichuan restaurant around thecorner.

表语关系 ​

The thing to be these days is a gold-collar.

同位语关系 ​

不定式短语对被修饰的名词作进一步补充说明。通常这类名词的动词形式或形容词形式与to搭配使用。

动词相关的有arrangement ，attempt，claim，decision，hope，failure，need，plan等

His refusal to co-operate makes us angry.
I have no wish to change.

形容词相关的有：ability ，ambition，anxiety，eagerness，inclination，reluctance等。

Her reluctance to accept his invitation upset him.
> Their eagerness to help pleased me.

当副词 ​

不定式充当状语，等同于状语从句的省略。其中，表目的最为常。表示其他状语时，通常会添加其他一些词，来与目的状语区分开。

目的状语 ​

不定式当副词的主要功能。一般而言，此时不定式的逻辑主语需要与句子的主语保持一致。

Hating people is like burning down your own house to get rid of a rat.

To study English well, a lot of practices are needed. // error

主语不一致时，可通过for sb指出。

I stopped and waited for her to catch up.

为了强调目的，可将to do放在首位，或用in order to do、so as to do等来强调。

To avoid criticism, do nothing, say nothing, be nothing.
I quote others in order better to express my own self.
We had better start early so as to catch the train.

to do目的状语的否定形式要用in order not to或so as not to，而不能单独用not to。

结果状语 ​

only to do

意为【结果却】，引出意想不到或不愉快的结果。有消极含义。

We hurried to the railway station, only to find the train had justleft.

so...as to do

意为【以至于】。so为副词，后接形容词或副词。可用such代替so + adj/adv。

Her story is so sad as to arouse our sympathy.
Her story is such as to arouse our sympathy.

as to的结构还有be such as to。

The pain in his foots wasn't such as to stop his walking.

...enough to do

enough为副词。此时不定式的逻辑主语可与句子的主语不一致。

The opening is big enough for the hand to slide in.

too...to

作目的状语时表达否定含义。否定含义来源于too，意为【太…而不能】。

The box is too heavy for me to even move.
The tea is too hot to drink.

too...to的否定形式有两种：not too...to do（不很...所以能），too...not to do（很...所以不会不）。

We are never too old to learn.
He is too smart not to see your point.

原因状语 ​

通常用来表示产生某种感情或评价的原因。

I was delighted to meet him.
He is happy for his daughter to marry a wealthy man.
The boy was rude to speak to his parents like that.

too...to

too...to可做原因状语，此时表示肯定含义。

一种形式是，too后面接表达意愿的形容词，表示【非常想】,【急于】之意。

I'm just too eager to help you.
You are too ready to find faults with other people.

另一种是在too前用only，all，but，really等修饰，表【非常】之意。

I am only too pleased to help you.

条件状语 ​

此时主句的谓语通常含有助动词，如will/would, must。

The father will be proud for his son to win the gold medal.
One must be out of his mind to do such a thing.
To look at him, you would like him.

虚拟语气

I would have been happy to be invited to the party.

独立成分 ​

不定式可以是一个完整的句子，来修饰其他句子。在整句中充当引导性的副词，同finally之类。常见的有：

to begin with（首先），to tell the truth（老实说），to make a long story short（长话短说），so to speak（可以这么说），to be brief/exact/frank/honest（简单地说/确切地说/坦率地说/坦白地说），to say nothing of （姑且不说），to say the least（至少可以这么说)。

To make a long story short, he is in hospital now.
I have a point there, to say the least.

引导句子 ​

不定式可引导独立的句子，表达愿望、担心、惊讶等强烈的情感。比如to think，to imagine，意为【你想想……竟有这样的事】。

To think she wanted to walk all the way to the park.
To spend so much money on something he didn't even need. // he should spend

时态与语态 ​

被动态 ​

一般来讲，在句中若有不定式动作的执行者，则不定式用主动形式；若没有执行者，则用被动形式。

I have something important to do.
It seems to be the only thing (for me) to do.

I have a lot of clothes to be washed. // 找人洗
He was afraid to be fooled.

同时主句谓语也是被动。

The book is not allowed to be taken out of the library.
The book is said to have been published. // 完成被动式

注意 to let 和 to blame 这两个短语是固定用法，主动形式表示被动意思。

You should be to blame for it.
The house is to let.

完成式 ​

表达发生在谓语动作之前的动作，或者表达过去虚拟。

I am sorry not to have come on Thursday. // 完成时表过去
= I am sorry that I didn't come on Thursday.

对比：

I'm sorry to keep you waiting. // 请稍等（将来的）
I'm sorry to have kept you waiting so long. // 久等了（过去的）

常见的后接完成式的动词有以下几类。(表达过去的场景)

态度情感：比如sorry，bless，happy...

We are happy to have visited so many historic places in Beijing.

观点：say，believe，think等，此时通常用于被动结构中。

Albert Einstein is estimated to have had the incredible intelligence quotient of 200. // 据估计

推断：seem，appear等。

It seems to have rained last night.

一般地，不定式的完成时用于表达过去，当然也可表达完成时态。

She said she was sorry to have missed you. // 过去完成
She hopes to have finished the work by the time he comes back. // 将来完成

也可以表达虚拟语气：【本想做但没做】。一般是表示【意愿】、【目的】的动词。

I meant to have telephoned, but I forgot.
I had meant to telephone, but I forgot. // 过去完成时
I was/would like to have telephoned, ....

进行式 ​

He pretended to be listening attentively when the teacher cameby.
I seem to have been learning English for over ten years.

adj to do ​

句式形容词+to do很常见，大部分可以理解为状语从句的省略。理解该句式的关键是注意形容词的特点——描述人或物的性质特点还是描述行为事件的特点。

结果状语 ​

形容词描述人或物的特征。

The book is interesting to buy.
= ... so you can buy it

原因状语 ​

此时的形容词主要用于描述个人情感或来自他人的评价。

评价性的形容词有brave，careful，generous，kind，clever等等。

You are foolish to spend so much.
He was wrong to tell lies.

表达情感的形容词有afraid，angry，anxious，unwilling，worried等等。

I was excited to be there.
He was most reluctant to lend us the money.

She's anxious for her daughter to win the competition.

条件（方式）状语 ​

此时的形容词多为说明事物的特征，后面的to do行为是产生该特征的条件或方式。

The coffee is bitter to taste.
The room is comfortable to live in.

The money is enough for you to buy books with. // 也可以理解为结果状语

描述行为 ​

上面介绍的句子，形容词是描述人或物的性质特点的。有一些句子中的形容词是描述行为特点的（去掉不定式后会不太正确）。这类句子可以理解为某种句子的变体。

1. 【他的动作是...的】

此时的形容词（的副词形式）是来修饰不定式的动作的，如slow，quick，prompt等等。

He is slow to react.
= He reacts slowly.

2. 【对它如此是...的】

此时形容词描述的是不定式的这个行为，如wonderful，difficult，impossible，safe，easy等等。句子主语充当不定式的宾语。

He is easy to fool.
= It is easy to fool him.

Mary is easy to get on with.
Football is very interesting to watch.

省略 to ​

宾补 ​

不定式作某些动词的宾语补足语，一般要省略to。这些动词包括：

使役动词：make，have，let。

感官动词：感（feel），听（listen to，hear），看（see，look at ,watch，notice，observe）

The teacher lets us write a composition every week.
I saw a man enter the shop.

若上述动词在被动结构时，其后的不定式需加 to。

A man was seen to enter the shop.

有些动词则可选的省略to。

The teacher will help you (to) study English well.

短语 ​

在某些副词性短语结构后，不用to。有cannot...but(不得不)，had better(最好)，might as well(不妨)，more than(不只是)，rather than(而不是)，other than(不同于)等等。

I cannot but admire his courage.
No one could do other than admire it. // 大家都推崇它

此外，某些固定搭配也是不定式省略to的形式。如make do(将就应付)，make believe(假装)，let drop，let fall，let fly(发射)，let slip，let go of(松开)，hear say(听说)，leave go of(放走)等等。

Let go of me! // 放开我

do ​

在某些句型中，如果前面含有do，则不用to。

有do nothing but do，nothing to do but do。

I have nothing to do but wait.
have no choice but to wait.

在解释 do 的精确含义的主语从句中，如：

All they could do is keep working.
> What you can do is wait and see.
> The thing to do now is clear up the mess.

why do that？ ​

特殊疑问词中，只有why后面要接省去to的不定式。意为【为什么要做某事？】，含有责怪之意。通常表明做某事是无意义或愚蠢的。

而否定形式的 Why not do sth? 则通常表示建议，在口语中非常常用。

并列结构 ​

用and，or，but等词连接表达并列的关系时，为了避免重复而把连词后面的不定式的to省去。

I'd like to lie down and go to sleep.
Do you want to have lunch now or wait till later?
I come to see you as well as learn the truth.

但若并列结构有对比强调的含义，to会被保留。

It is the greatest happiness on earth to love and to be loved.
He likes to be respected, not to respect others.

逻辑主语 ​

不定式的动作实施者。

for sb to do：最主要的结构，在句子中可充当多种成分。

He was very willing for everyone else to come.
It's important for you to go there in person.

It is adj of sb to do：与描述性格特征或行为表现的形容词搭配，表达【他是...的，因为...】。可以认为是原因状语的adj to do的另一种表达。

It's generous of him to lend me his car.
He is generous to lend me his car.

动名词 ​

名词类。

可以认为，动名词由名词从句省略而来，带有【确定性】、【正在进行】或【持续性】的语气。

作主语 ​

动名词作主语一般直接放在句首，常泛指某些动作或行为。谓语动词常用单数。

Hating people is like burning down your own house to get rid of a rat.
> Climbing and diving are my favourite sports.

在某些句型中动名词可放在句尾。

it is no good/use doing sth、there is no point/use/good (in) doing sth：干某事没有用

It's not much use my buying salmon if you don't like fish.
There is no use your arguing with him.

there is no doing sth：做某事是不可能的

There's no knowing the future.

作宾语 ​

在某些动词后面一般需接动名词，如 admit,advise,anticipate,appreciate,avoid,consider,contemplatedelay,deny,detest,discuss,dislike,encourage,endure,enjoy,escape,excuse,fancy,favor,feel like,finish,forbid,forgive,give up,can'thelp,imagine,involve,leaveoff,mention,mind,miss,overlook,permit,postpone,practice,prohibit,putoff,resent,resist,risk,can't stand,suggest,tolerate,understand

非谓语总结 ​

there be ​

there be 的非谓语形式依然表达【有】的意思。

其不定式形式为there to be。

I expect there to be no argument about this.
