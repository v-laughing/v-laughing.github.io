语言的基本功能是描述对象的行为与特征。

否定句 ​

1. ** 谓语中含有be 动词或情态动词时，在其后面加not**。

2. 谓语动词是实义动词时，借助助动词do来否定：do/does not 动词原型。

He does not like English.
I did not like English.

一般疑问句 ​

询问是否是某件事。肯定回答用yes，否定回答用no。

1. 谓语中含有be 动词或情态动词时，将其移到句首。

Is he a teacher?
> Can you swim?

2. 谓语动词是实义动词时，将do的形式放置句首。

Do you like English?
> Does not he like English?

特殊疑问句 ​

询问具体的某个事物。句式为：特殊疑问词 一般疑问句 ?

1. 询问哪个人。

Who can sing in English?
> Who did you see at the party last night?

2. 询问哪个事。

What are you studying?

3. 询问什么时候。

When were you born?

4. 询问在哪里。

Where does he live?

5. 询问怎样做的。

How does he go to school?

6. 询问为什么。

Why do you often study at the library?
> Why are you going to the bank after class?

7. 询问哪一个什么东西。

Which pen do you want?

7. 询问谁的什么东西。

Whose book do you want?

8. 询问某事的数量与程度。用how adj/adv。

How tall is he?
> How well does he speak English? // 说得怎么样

9. 询问动作发生的频率。

How often do you write to your parents?
> How many times a month do you write to your parents?

回答时可采用如下短语。

基本句型 ​

主系表 ​

表达主语性质特征。

主要的系动词有，be 动词、look、sound、smell、taste、feel、seem、appear、become、turn。表语结构通常为名词或形容词。

He became a teacher.
The leaf will turn yellow in autumn.

主谓 ​

发生的动作没有作用对象，谓语动词是不及物动词。

Internet dating hurts.
He works hard.

主谓宾 ​

发生的动作有直接的作用对象，谓语动词是及物动词。

I love English.
They are looking for one-minute solutions toeverything.
This attitude leads to disappointment or even failure.

主谓+双宾 ​

双宾结构。表达【对某人做某事】。

前者是间接宾语，通常是人，表动作的接收者。前者是直接宾语，通常是物，表动作的承受者。

I gave him my book. // gave my book to him.
He showed the guard his passport.

主谓宾+宾补 ​

表达【使某事物有怎样的状态或特征】。宾语补足语表达宾语的状态与特征。

We elected John chairman.
You can leave the door open.

名词从句 ​

把句子当作名词来用，充当另一句话的主语、宾语、表语或同位语。陈述句、一般疑问句和特殊疑问句都可以充当名词。

陈述句作名词：需在句首加上that，以使得整体的句子结构保持正确。句子充当宾语时可省略that。

That the seas are being overfished has been known for years.

一般疑问句作名词：需将疑问句的倒装语序变成陈述句语序，并在句首加上whether 或 if 来引导。

I don't know whether or not he comes.

if 一般只用于引导宾语从句，且不和 or not 直接连用。

特殊疑问句作名词：变倒装语序为陈述语序。如果疑问词本身在名词从句中充当主语，则无需调整语序结构。

Who will chair the meeting has not yet been decided.

主语从句 ​

从句在句子中充当主语。

That the world is round is a fact.
> Whether he comes or not makes no difference to me.
> What we need is wholly new kind of police force.
> How a person masters his fate is more important than what his fateis.

陈述句作主语时，一种常见的形式是用it 作形式主语置于句首，而将主语从句放在句末。

It is a fact that the world is round.

这种句式包括了许多的所谓的“重点句型”，如It's reported that..(据报道)，It is likely that...(很可能)，It is an honor that...(真荣幸)等等。

宾语从句 ​

从句在句子中充当宾语。

We know the world is round. // 省略 that
I don't know if/whether he needs my help.
I have not decided whom I should vote for.
We don't know why he did not come yesterday.

表语从句 ​

从句在句子中充当表语。

My idea is that the child should be sent to school.
My concern is whether he comes or not.
The point is when you will become rich.

同位语从句 ​

同位语，即用一个完整的句子来补充说明某个名词。

The rumor that Tom was a thief turned out to be untrue.

同位语从句可以与其修饰的名词被其他成分隔开。

A saying goes that practice makes perfect.

对于疑问句，所修饰的先行词通常是problem，idea等等。

There arose the question where we could get the loan.
They are faced with the problem whether they should continue to work.

定语从句 ​

句子充当形容词，来修饰名词或名词短语。被修饰的名词称为先行词。连接主句和从句，并且在定语从句中充当某个成分的连接词称为关系词。

I don't like people who never keep their word.

通常，关系词在从句中充当主语、宾语或表语。

I like guys who have a good sense of humor. // 主语
He is the best grammar teacher who I have ever seen. // 宾语
I like the people who I work with. // 介词后宾语

充当主语时，从句的谓语动词形式由关系词所指代的先行词来决定。

充当宾语时，关系词可省略。

关系代词 ​

在定语从句里充当主语或宾语（whose 作定语）。

who & whom ​

先行词指人时。who是主格形式，前面不能与介词搭配使用。whom是宾格形式，不能在从句中充当主语。

I'm talking about friends who you can share almost everything with.
I'm talking about friends with whom you can share almosteverything.

whose ​

表示人或物的所有关系，可指人或物。其后要接名词，【whose +名词】在从句中可作主语、宾语。

These children sit in a schoolroom whose windows are all broken.
It was a meeting whose importance I did not realize at the time.

which ​

代指物。先行词可为短语或句子。

Anger is a wind which blows out the lamp of the mind.
He likes climbing mountains, which is a good exercise.
> He tore up my photo, which upset me.

that ​

可代指人或物。代指物时可替换为which，代指人时可替换为who。不用在非限制性定语从句中，不能用于介词的后面。

下面几种情况应该用that。

先行词中同时出现【人】和【物】时

The writer and his novels that the article deals with are quitefamiliar to us.

先行词为指物的 all, little, few, much, none 和 the first 时

his book contains little that is useful. // 几乎没用
> All that glitters is not gold. // 并非都是

先行词是不定代词 something, anything, nothing 和 everything 时

** 先行词被限定词，如 any, only, the only, the one，或序数词，最高级等成分修饰时**

There is no difficulty that they can't overcome.
> The only thing that we should do is find our way home.
> The very problem that I want to solve is like this.

关系副词 ​

在定语从句里充当状语。

when ​

代指时间状语。

I'll never forget the day when I met you.
My favorite season is spring, when the earth seems born again.

并非先行词为时间相关时，就用when。用when时其必须在从句中充当状语。

I'll never forget the time which I spent on campus. // 宾语

where ​

代指地点状语。

This is the town where I spent my childhood.
> The library, which was built in the 1930's, needs to be renovated. // 主语

why ​

表示原因，只引导限制性定语从句。

This is the reason why I didn't come here. // 等同于 for which

非限制定语从句 ​

限制与非限制定语从句的区别在结构上表现为是否有逗号分隔。

限制性定语从句是用来对一个名词提供必要的信息。从句若去掉，尽管句子结构正确，但语义不清晰。

He is a man who is of value to the people.

非限制性定语从句用来提供附加的而非必要的信息，对先行词作进一步的解释、补充。

一般地，当先行词为专有名词或泛指名词时，可用非限制定语从句。

My mother, who is 50 this year, lives with me now.
> Hawaii, which consists of eight principal islands, is a favoritevacation spot.
> An elephant, which is the earth's largest land mammal, hasfew natural enemies other than human beings.

句意差别 ​

正如名字所言，限制性定语从句强调被限制的那部分先行词，隐含着存在其他的不在限制范围的先行词。

The food which wasn't in the fridge all went off.
那些没有放在冰箱里的食物坏了。

非限制定语从句不起到限制作用，仅仅是补充说明，起到状语的含义，如原因、条件、让步。

The food, which wasn't in the fridge, all went off. // 原因
The millionaire had another house built, which he didn't need atall. // 让步（虽然）

区分：同位语从句 ​

结构上，that在定语从句中要充当成分，而在同位语从句中不作任何成分，仅起连词的作用。

语义上，定语从句相当于形容词，对先行词起修饰、描述和限制的作用；同位语从句相当于一个名词，对其前面名词内容的具体表述。

The news that you heard is not true. // 那个消息
> The news that Lincoln was murdered at a theater is true. // 有消息称

状语从句 ​

时间状语 ​

时间从句所表达的时间通常是过去和将来。

when ​

when 可用表示at that time，此时从句的谓语动词通常是短暂动词。

表达过去时，从句可用过去完成时来强调从句动作的先发生。

I started my dinner when he left.
I started my dinner when he had left.

表达将来时，主句用一般将来时，从句用一般现在时。

I will tell him about it when he comes back.

when也可表达during the time，强调一个短暂动作发生在一个延续的动作中。此时从句接延续动词。

The doorbell rang when I was telephoning.
门铃响了，那时我正在打电话

也可用用另一种方式表达 TODO。

I was telephoning when suddenly the doorbell rang.
正在打电话，这时门铃突然响了

while ​

while 用作时间连词时，可表达during that time。

The phone rang while I was taking my bath.

until ​

until 所表达的意思是，一个（主句）动作一直持续到某一个时间点，因此其主句的谓语应是延续性的，而从句谓语是短暂性的。

Until I came back, he was waiting for me at my home.
We'll stay here until it stops raining.

until 也可作为介词使用，后面接时间点。

I will be away from my office until next Tuesday. // 状态

until 的主句可接否定的短暂性动词，因其表达一个状态，即 not...until 结构。

He didn't leave the office until his boss came back.
直到回来后，才离开办公室。

I didn't go to bed until 12 o'clock last night.

一...就 ​

有一些时间连词来表达主句动作和从句动作相继发生。

从句先于主句：一般有as soon as, once, the minute, the moment, immediately, directly和instantly等。

We will leave as soon as it stops raining. // 将来
It began to rain immediately I arrived home. // 过去

主句先于从句：一般有hardly...when, scarcely...when和no sooner...than。这种结构一般表示过去的动作，此时，主句多用过去完成时，且常倒装，从句用一般过去时。

No sooner had I arrived home than it began to rain.
> Hardly had the storm started when all the lights went out.

地点状语 ​

通常是由where引导，来表达主句的动作发生的场所。

Stay where you are. // 呆在原处
A driver should slow down where there are school.

可放在句首，表达抽象的【地点】。

Where there is a will, there is a way. // 有志者，事竟成

原因状语 ​

常用的连词有because, for, as和since。

because ​

回应why的语句，引出那些听话人不知道或最感兴趣的话题。可被强调词如only, just和perhaps等修饰。

My friends dislike me just because I'm handsome and successful.

since ​

指出已知的事实，常译为【既然】，通常放在句首。

Since we don't have class tomorrow, why not go out for a picnic?

as ​

与since的含义差不多，指出在说话人看来已经很明显的事实。

As Monday is a national holiday, all government offices will beclosed.

for ​

给出一种推断，对前面分句的内容加以解释或说明。

It rained last night, for the ground is wet this morning.

介词 ​

也可用介词短语来表达原因（because含义），常见的介词有because of, due to和owing to。owing to常置于句首。

We had an accident due to his carelessness.

目的状语 ​

引出目的，从句中常含有情态动词。连词有so that, in order that。

I turned off the TV in order that my roommate could study inpeace and quiet.

结果状语 ​

有so，so/such that，译为【所以】或【以至于】。其从句都要放在主句之后，而不能提到句首。

so that ​

so 后面可接形容词或副词。

I was so late that I didn't have time to get on it.

such that ​

such的后面要接名词。

He is such a good teacher that everyone likes him.

such/so that引导的状语从句，为了强调，可将so或such引导的部分置于句首从而构成倒装结构。

So fast does light travel that it is difficult for us to imagine itsspeed.
> Such a fool was he that he believed her.

条件状语 ​

主要有if和unless，译为【如果】、【否则】。

If winter comes, can spring be far behind?
these will be useless unless you carry them out.

其他的连词还有：suppose/supposing/providing/provided（that）（如果），so/as long as， on condition that（只要）。

So long as you work hard enough, an iron rod can be groundinto a needle.
Knowledge helps you to reach the destination provided you know what the destination is.

让步状语 ​

表达【虽然】、【尽管】、【即使】。常见的有though, although, even though/if。

Although he tried hard, (yet /still) he failed.

当然，也可通过介词来表达，如despite, in spite of和for all。

In spite of his inexperience, he did a very good job.
> Despite what he achieved in medicine, he remained modest.

通过省略的方式，连词后可直接接形容词或分词。

Though tired, he wouldn't stop working.

while ​

置于句首的 while 可以表示【尽管】。

While often praised by foreigners for its emphasis on the basics,Japanese education tends to stress test taking and mechanical learningover creativity and self⁃expression.

as ​

as 引导的倒装句可表示让步。

Young as he is, he is knowledgeable.
> Much as I respect him, I can not agree with him.
> Praised as he was, he remained modest.
> Lazy a boy as he is, he is kind to help others.

比较状语 ​

as...as ​

意为【如同...】，前一个as为副词，后一个as可作为连词或介词。

The work is not as difficult as you think.
Eyes are as eloquent as lips.

第一个as后可接形容词+名词。

Let us take as simple an example as the addressing of envelopes. // 像信封地址这样的一个简单例子

在否定句中第一个as可用 so 代替。

This room is not so large as the one we saw yesterday.

为了保持句子平衡，从句还可以用倒装结构。

The computer revolution may well change society as fundamentally as did the Industrial Revolution.

than ​

用于同类事物对比，且主句中需出现比较级形式。

The weather of the South is wetter than that of the North.
He is taller than I.

同样的，从句可以用倒装结构。

On the whole, ambitious students are more likely to succeed intheir studies than are those with little ambition.

倍数关系 ​

可以用下列三种句型来表示。

as...as： A is three times as big as B

There are only half as many fisheries as there were 15 years ago.

than： A is three times bigger than B

of： A is three times the size of B

the more...the more ​

这种结构实质是分句的比较级部分的提到了句首，即more...结构必须在分句中充当成分。前半部分是从句，后半部分是主句。

The more exactly you use your words, the more easily people will understand you. // 副词
> The more we help others, the more we receive in return.
> The hotter the market is, the more risk there is.

方式状语 ​

表示动作的方式，意为【像】，【如同】。主要有as, like, as if, as though和the way。

When in Rome, do as the Romans do.
Work like you don't need the money.
He looks as though/if he is an actor.
Do it the way you were taught.
