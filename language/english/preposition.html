表示对象之间的关系。大部分介词的本义是描述空间关系，在这个基础上引申出其他的关系。

句法作用 ​

介词可以是单个单词，也可以是短语，如in front of，at the center of。部分分词也可以当成介词使用，如respecting，considering。

介词通常与名词搭配构成介词短语，在句中常充当定语或状语。

做名词 ​

Make yourself at home. // 宾补
He come out from behind the door. // 介词宾语

形容词 ​

A friend in need is a friend indeed. // 定语
He is always among the first to arrive. // 表语

副词 ​

He was away for over a year. // 时间
The weather changes with the seasons. // 伴随
> As a rule, our academic year begins in September. // 修饰句子
They treated me with respect and tender. // 程度
Nothing could live without air and water. // 让步

介词也可后接形容词，通常构成某些副词性的短语，或存在某些固定搭配中。

副词性短语 ​

at best(至多)，in brief(简言之)，in short(总之)，for good(永远)。

固定搭配 ​

We consider the wire as disconnected.
His explannation is far from satisfactory.
She is sort of excited.
He is anything but angry.

省略 ​

对于一些常用的短语，经常会在不影响语义的前提下省略介词。

of ​

与age/colour/height/use/length/size等搭配时，可省略of。特别地，at the age of ten可变为at age ten。

They were (of) the same age.
What does he want with a house (of) that size.
I think people (of) the age find that comforting.

for ​

【非正式】表示时间长度时。

The meeting lasted (for) three days.
I have lived in this neighbourhood (for) a long time.

在某些单词后接距离时，如walk，run，drive。

They travelled (for) ten miles.
The road extends (for) hundreds of miles.

on ​

后接特定日子时。

I will see you (on) Christmas Day.

to ​

在nearer/nearest to结构中省略。

Our hotel is the one nearest (to) beach.

at ​

在at about表时间时。

I picked him up (at) about dust.
We arrived (at) about ten o'colck.

by ​

在by mail，by express，by first class结构时。

ing 分词前 ​

常见于口语。

I have a difficult (in) speaking English.
They stopped him (from) attacking the loaded carts.

表现意义 ​

时间 ​

at：将所表述的时间视为一个时间点，忽略其长度意义。常见搭配有at ten o'clock， at noon/night，at sunset，at the moment，at the beginning/end of，at the age of等等。

in：强调时间长度，通常与持续性动词搭配；忽略具体的时间点，用长时间代替某个时间点，与短暂性动词搭配。

He was born in April 2006.
We either watch TV or read book in the evening.

in可表达将来：与短暂性动词搭配，作【在...之后】；与持续性动词搭配，作【在...之内】。

I will arrive in two hours.
The products will be produced in a month.

搭配有：in time(及时)，

on ：介于at，in之间。不将时间视为一个时间点，也不刻意突出时间长度。常用于某些特定描述的时间，如节日，重大事件等。

He died on the eve of victory.
She will come to meet us on his arrival.

搭配有：on time(准时)

by：可表达【在...之前】或【在...时候】。

Jack has some friends by the time you came.
He worked by day and slept by night.

搭配有：by then(到那时)

for：表示持续的时间长度，或【为了在某时（的场所）】。

I have been here for three days.
I would like to book a room for Friday night.
What would you like for your birthday?.

until：表【直到...为止】。

He did not stop working until late at night.

through：表【在整个...期间】，同throughout。

May the joy of the New Year be with you through the year.

over：表【在...期间】，不同于through，其不强调整个过程都参与。

Will you stay here over Christmas.

后接事物，可表【边...边...】。

We discuss the business over tea.

空间 ​

一般来说，at、on、on、in 可分别表达所指空间的点、线、面、体。

Let's meet at the railway station.
Then we can go over the project on our way to Gaoxiong.
Several boats can be seen on the lake.
It's cool in the railway station. (指在火车站里面)

固定搭配有：

at：接门牌号（at 115 fuzhou Road），出入口（at the front door）

in：in a line, in the world, in a picture。

on：坐落在...旁边（on the sea），是...的成员（on the football team），on the left, on the page。

at 强调所在地点发生的事，in 描述地点本身。

We had dinner at the hotel.
It’s always cold in Helen’s house.

交通工具：on a bus/train/plane/ship/bike, in a car/taxi

附近 ​

near：表相对的【近】。

There is a post office near our school.

by，beside：表【在...旁边】，beside比by更具体。

There is a big tree by the river.
He was sitting beside her.

next to：【紧挨着】。

The man next to Bill was talking to him in Spanish. （做定语）

方位 ​

in指在某范围内，表【在...之中】；to指在某范围外，常表【在...以东/西】；on表【毗邻】。

Guangdong lies in the south of China.
Japan lies to the east of China.
Vietnam lies on the south of China.

方向 ​

表达【朝...方向】的用法有：

in突出【体】，常与direction搭配；to突出【动作】；on突出【面】，表【正对着】；at突出【点】，表【对准】；for突出【目的】。

They will go in the same direction.
The road bends to the right.
My window looks on the street.
The dog ran at the cat.
A train is leaving Shanghai for Beijing.

上下 ​

above/below：相对位置的高低，不一定位于正上或正下方。

He lifted his hands above his head.
The dead sea is below the sea level.

也可表达在价格，数量，地位的相对高低。

She values safety above everything.
The price of house is below the past level.

over/under：有位置上的【垂直】，【遮蔽】含义。可描述年龄，数量，程度，百分比上的高低。

The clouds hangs over the summit.
We rested under the big tree.
Children over twelve must pay the full fare.

其余的表达有：over【从上方越过】；under【在指导/照顾之下】。

The dog jumped over the fense.
We learned a great deal under his teaching.

on/beneath：强调表面的接触。

They sat on the grass, talking and laughing.
He wore a woollen vest beneath his shirt.

通过 ​

across：本义为【在二维平面上，从一边到另一边】，即【穿过】。延申意有，【与...交叉】、【在...对面】、【越过】、【在...两边】。

It take a few hours to fly across the country.
At one point the railway line goes across the road.
The bus stop is just across the street.
A smile of pleasure passed across his face.
The shirt is too tight across the shoulders.

through：本义为【从内部穿过】，即【从...通过】。

The sunlight was coming in through the window.
On our way we need to pass through the Hudosn Street.

by：可以表【经由】、【经过某地】。

She crossed the stream by a little bridge.
The next flight goes to Rome by way of Paris.

在...之中/之间 ​

among：本义是【处于多个物体之中】，含有被围绕的意思。延申义有【是...中的一个】。

There is a cottage among the trees.
Shelly is among the world's greatest poets.

与反身代词搭配，表【相互间】。

They quarrelled among themselves.

between：本义为【在两个物体中间】，结构为between A and B。

He is a man between fifty and sixty.

当仅仅接复数名词时，可表多方中两两之间的关系。

Agreements were made between diffrent countries.

方式 ​

by：表【通过】，指借助某种方式做某事，后接一般或抽象的名词，如交通工具等。

We often met by appointment.
You can send letter by email.
They came by air and left by ship.

常见的搭配有：by nature(就本性而言)，by birth(论血统上)，by profession(以职业的方式)，by contact(以承包的方式)。

in：表【用】，通常后接语言，way。后接服装时，表【穿戴】。

He often encourage us to talk in English in class.
Only in this way can you slove the problem.
The three men in school uniform are running.

through：表【通过】，含有【贯穿全程】的意思。

I obtained my position by a friend.
He succeeded through hard work.

with：表【用，以】，一般后接具体的名词。其他的意思有：【拿着】、【和...】、【随着，做伴随状语】。

I like to write a lettle with a ball-pen.
A young woman came in with a cup of coffee.
He has broken with his friends.
He was sleep with his head on his arms.

常见搭配有：with practice，with patience。

原因 ​

through：指因疏忽，不慎等原因。常与neglect，carelessness搭配。

He cut himself through carelessness.

with：指外部影响内部的原因。

The little girl was shivering with cold.

from：指造成疲劳，痛苦，死亡的原因，常在某些单词后面。

She felt tired from a whole day's work.
The problem resulted from a diffrence of opinion.

at：描述某些感情的原因，意为【当听到/看到/想到...】，即at the sound/sight/thought of。

She look surprised at seeing us.

其他描述原因的介词有：for，due to，owing to，because of，on account of，thanks to。

due to可做表语，其他一般仅作状语。thanks to一般做【多亏，幸亏】之意。

Our delay is due to traffic jam.
> Owing to his lack of exprience, John failed to do the work well.
> Thanks to your kind help, we finished early.

度量 ​

at：描述速度，频率，价格等。

The train runs at about 120 miles an hour.
Strawberries sell at a high price in winter.

by：by the + 计量单位(单数)表【以...为计】；by + 复数数词表【成千上百地】。

Meat is sold by pound.
They sat down in ranks by hundreds. // 100 人一排

表示面积尺寸。

The hole high up in the wall is about 19 cm by 18 cm.

表示年龄，距离，数量等的【以...之差】。

He was my senior by two years in the university.
The bullet missed my head by a matter of inches.

表【增减程度】。

Industrial production has risen by nearly two-fifths in the past three years.

其他非表示度量的含义有：【按照】，如by law（依据法律）。

for：可作【以...数量的钱】。与every搭配时，可表示【比例】。

He sold his house for about 2 million dollars.
> For every three good apples, there is a bad one.

比较 ​

as：【作为】。

He is working as a waiter during the vacation.
Use your payroll number as a password for the computer.

like：【像...一样】。

They were behaving like children.

against：【对比于...】，如利率，the rate of A agianst B。

we have six girl students in our class this year agianst ten last year.

延申义有：【在...背景下】。

The pine trees were black against the morning sky.

从属 ​

of

of + 抽象名词有形容词特性，作后置定语或形容词，用于描述特征。

He is a man of wisdom. // a wise man
The matter is of great importance. // is very important

与一些常见的抽象名词搭配时，of可被省略。

It is (of) no use learnig without pratice.

数词类 + of + 名词表达【部分】，如none/some/one-half of them。

名词A + of a + 名词B表示【...般的】，如a angle of a wife，a poem of a night。

with： 【具有】，接具体名词，表示具有某个特征。

I have a new coat with four pockets.

for：接具体名词，可表【...的】。

After dinner I received a bill for $90.
> The British term for baggage is luggage.

让步 ​

in spite of：用法同despite，后接动名词或名词，接句子时需加the fact that。

He went to work dispite his illness.
> In spite of the fact that she fell midway, she won the race.

for all：同with all，意为【虽有种种】。

For all his wealth, he is unhappy.

常见搭配有：for all I care(与我无关)，for all I know(据我所知)。

条件 ​

as for：【至于，说到】，后接名词或动名词。as to与其同义，但可接名词性从句。

As for being shy, you will get over that.
She had no idea at all as to what she ought to do.

for：【至于，对于】。前面为形容词时，可表达惊讶的感情，即【就...来说的话】。

He had a natural gift for teaching.
His knowledge is unbelievable for a boy of eighteen.

in terms of：【就...来说】。

In terms of money they are quite rich, but not in terms of happiness.

without：【没有...的话】。

you cannot get in without a pass.

目的 ​

for：【为了】。

She went to France for holiday.
He would do anything for her.

for fear of：【唯恐，担心】，有【为了避免】之意。

We walking softly for fear of waking the others.

for the sake of：【为了...的（利益）】。也可简化为for sb's/sth's sake。

You must wear a belt in the car for the sake of safety.

除外 ​

besides：【除...外，还有】，义同in addition to、including。

There were three other people at the meeting besides Mr. Day.

except：【除去...外】，后可接名词，副词，从句等各种成分。

We looked everywhere except here.
My mother usually goes to shop by bike except when it rains.

接不定式时，若主句中带do，则可省略to。

Mary does everything except wash the car.
It has no effect except to make him angry.

用在句首时，往往在后接for。

Except for this, everything is in order.

except for：【除了有...之外】，表达小的例外情况。

The composition is very good except for a few spelling mistakes.

but：【除了...外】，义同except。except 强调排除部分，but强调整体部分。常与all，anyone搭配。

Everyone know it but you.
> Who but Jack would do such a thing?

but for：【要不是】，常用于虚拟语气。

But for the rain, we would have a pleasant journey.

介词辨析 ​

made of / made from ：由...组成。前者表示成分与主体的关系很直接，容易”看出“，后者则相反。

between/among：在...之间。前者由标示位置的功能，后者则没有。

Taizhong lies between Taibei and Tainan. (在两位置之间)

throw to/throw at：扔向...。前者强调方向，后者强调某点。

from...to/from...through：前者界定较为模糊，后者较为清晰，包含头尾。

above/over：超过。前者表相对高度超过，即比较高；后者带有指定的标示，只在...上方。类似的词组还有below/under。

Mt.Everest soars above all other peaks in the Alps.

into & out of ​

方向性介系词。介系词短语作形容词或副词。

to：go to work，come to the house，return to London。

into/out of：go into the room, go out of the car。

on/off：go on/off the bus

与home搭配时常常省略介词：go/arrive home，on the way home
